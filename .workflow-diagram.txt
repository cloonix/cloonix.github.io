WHAT THE SCRIPT DOES:
=====================

Input:  ~/Documents/blog-drafts/my-post.md
        └── assets/screenshot.png

        ↓ [validate front matter]
        ↓ [generate filename: 20251225_my_post.md]
        ↓ [resize screenshot.png if > 1920px]
        ↓ [update image paths in markdown]
        ↓ [copy to Hugo directories]

Output: content/blog/20251225_my_post.md
        static/images/blog/20251225_my_post/screenshot.png

        ↓ [move draft to published/]

Result: ~/Documents/blog-drafts/published/my-post.md
